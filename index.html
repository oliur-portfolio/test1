Frequently Asked Questions

Is delivery free?

Absolutely! Youll be able to take care of your copay through our app or over the phone, but there are no additional charges for delivery.

How do refills work?

We manage your refills and send them to you on time so you shouldnt miss a dose. Well keep track of dates, so you dont have to! We'll coordinate with your doctor and your insurance company to make sure you never run out of medication.

Do you offer alternative packaging options?

From blister packs to easy open pill bottles, we offer a variety of different packaging options that make it easier for you to keep track of your medications and take them on time. To learn more or to try one of our different packaging options, give us a call at (212) 369-1350.

How can I get in touch with a pharmacist?

Request a consultation through our mobile app, or get in touch via phone, email, or text, and well be in touch soon!

How much will my prescription cost?

Depending on your insurance and your medication, we determine your copay and deductible (if any) beforehand. Our team applies financial assistance wherever possible and is always available to answer additional questions about payment.

How do I pay for my prescriptions?

All payments can be made via our Medly mobile app, over a phone call with our support team, or with your driver at the time of hand-off. We accept all major credit cards and cash at time of delivery.

What forms of insurance do you accept?

We accept all major insurances, including Medicaid, Medicare, and private insurance. If you have questions, feel free to call us at (212) 369-1350 and we'll be happy to help.

I was a patient at Blake Pharmacy before it was
changed to Medly Pharmacy. Are you able to fill the prescriptions I had with Blake?

Yes! If you were a patient of Blake Pharmacy, Medly will continue to provide you with the same exceptional service you have come to expect. Existing customers can still tap into the expertise of their local pharmacy walking in to order and pick up medications if you so choose but now you will also have access to the digital support and benefits that Medly offers, too. If you have additional questions, please reach out to Medly Pharmacy at (212) 369-1350 and well be happy to help.

I am a provider that sent my patients prescriptions
to Blake Pharmacy before it was changed to Medly Pharmacy. How can I get in touch with your team to make sure there are no disruptions with my patients medications?

Providers will still use the same phone numbers to get in touch with Medly Pharmacy. You may reach out to us via phone (212) 369-1350 or fax (212) 369-2212. We will continue to communicate with you and your patients regarding any changes throughout this transition.
